# Privacy Policy for Kanji Sensei: Play & Learn

Effective Date: June 23, 2026

## 1. Introduction
We take your privacy—and especially the privacy of young learners—very seriously. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information in the "Kanji Sensei: Play & Learn" mobile application for Android.

## 2. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)
This application is designed for children under the age of 13, specifically primary school students learning Japanese Kanji. 
- We strictly comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).
- We do NOT knowingly collect, store, track, request, or share any personally identifiable information from children.

## 3. Data Collection and Usage
- **Personal Data:** This app collects ZERO personal data. No accounts are required, and no registration is necessary to play.
- **Device & Usage Data:** The app runs entirely offline. We do not use analytical SDKs, background tracking, crash reporters, or performance counters that transmit data over the web.
- **Permissions:** The app does not request access to dangerous device permissions such as your camera, microphone, contacts, storage files, or fine GPS location.

## 4. Advertising and Third-Party Services
To maintain a safe, clean educational workspace for primary demographics, this application is completely AD-FREE. There are no third-party marketing frameworks or ad networks integrated into our software bundle.
